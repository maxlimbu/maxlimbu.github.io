Instructions

This is a simple squash game where you get points when you hit the bat everytime after the ball has been retreived. It has similar concept to tennis rebound practise where the aim of the game is to last as long as possible without letting the ball go. Everytime a point is earned the ball will slowly get faster as you get more points.

Key Controls

You may wish to move the bat with either the mouse or the keyboard left(<-) and right arrow(->) key to secure the ball with the bat so it dowsnot drop to the floor.
